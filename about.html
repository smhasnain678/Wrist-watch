ABOUT WZ
Since 1950
International Watch Company

WatchZone is a legacy of astounding four generations. Over seven decades, WatchZone has been recognized as official authorized retailer of more than sixteen international luxury watch brands including Rolex. We aspire to provide our customers best buying experience and are
dedicated to maintain the nobility and prestige of your timepiece by delivering finest after sales services. WatchZone is a family-owned enterprise, deep rooted in 1950 by G.K Shehzada, who has been passionated about time keeping and horology since childhood. All watches are sold at Website and outlets with international and local warranty. We are proud to have official outlets in ten major cities all over the country.
